# RevenueCat

Monetize your app with native subscriptions powered by RevenueCat.

RevenueCat simplifies in-app subscription handling, freeing you from managing receipts, renewal logic, and App Store / Google Play quirks manually. NativeLaunch comes pre-integrated with RevenueCat and includes a **fully customizable paywall screen**.

* * *

## Subscription Model

NativeLaunch supports **auto-renewable subscriptions** as the only monetization type. These allow users to access premium features continuously until they cancel their plan.

Features:

-   ✅ Cross-platform (iOS + Android) subscription handling
-   ✅ No server setup required
-   ✅ Trials & intro pricing support
-   ✅ Real-time revenue analytics
-   ✅ Easily test and update subscriptions via dashboard

* * *

## Built-in Paywall

NativeLaunch includes a ready-to-use paywall UI:

-   Displays products from RevenueCat dynamically
-   Shows prices, trial periods, and terms
-   Handles purchase loading states, errors, and success
-   Customizable design via your app’s styles
-   Supports multiple products and offering IDs

## Why RevenueCat?

-   ✅ Handles iOS + Android in-app purchases with unified API
-   🔐 Built-in receipt validation and subscription tracking
-   📊 Access to real-time analytics and customer dashboards

NativeLaunch handles the integration, so all you need is to define your products in App Store / Play Console and RevenueCat.
